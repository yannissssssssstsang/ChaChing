Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StallMate. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

2. Data Storage & Ownership

StallMate is designed with a "Portable Shop State" architecture. This means:

  • Your Data is Yours: All inventory, sales records, and settings are stored directly in your personal Google Drive account.
  • No Central Database: We do not maintain a central database of your business transactions or customer information.
  • Local Storage: Temporary data is stored in your browser's LocalStorage for performance and offline capabilities.

3. Google API Usage

StallMate requests access to specific Google services to provide its core functionality:

  • Google Drive: Used to save and sync your shop data. We only access files created by StallMate or files you explicitly select.
  • Gmail: Used solely to send digital receipts to your customers at your request. We do not read your emails.
  • Google Identity: Used to authenticate you and connect to your Drive/Gmail services.

4. AI Processing

When you use our AI features (like inventory extraction from images), the images are processed via the Gemini API. These images are used only for the immediate extraction task and are not stored by StallMate after processing.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Leaflet for mapping features and Recharts for analytics. These libraries run locally in your browser and do not receive your private business data.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us through our official support channels.
